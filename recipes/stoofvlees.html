Flemish stew with fries

Description

A classic Flemish dish.

Ingredients

Stew

  • Pork cheeks 1 kg
  • Large onion 2
  • Bread 1 slice
  • Luikse siroop 2 tbsp
  • Chicken spices
  • Red or brown beer 2 bottles
  • Mustard 2 tbsp
  • Dried bay leaves 4
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Fries

  • Potatoes 1 kg
  • Salt

Side dish

  • Chicory 4
  • Apple 1
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

Stew

  1. Dice the onions and stew the shortly in a big pot.
  2. Rub the chicken spices on the pork cheeks. Heat up a pan and sear the pieces of meat on both side until brown. Add the pieces to the pot with the onions.
  3. Deglaze the pan with the beer and also add to the pot. Add water until all the meat is fully submerged.
  4. Add the Luikse siroop and dried bay leaves.
  5. Spread the mustard on the bread slice and put in the pot on top of the meat with the spread on the bottom. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Let the stew simmer for 3 hours and stir once in a while.

Side dish

  1. Rinse the chicory and remove the heart. Cut in to long and very fine strips.
  2. Do the same with the apple.
  3. Add the chicory and apple to a mixing bowl with the mayonnaise. Season with salt and pepper.

Fries

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes in fries. Do not rinse the fries.
  2. Heat up the fryer until 140 degrees Celcius.
  3. Fry the fries, but do not let them get any color. Let them rest in a bowl with a piece of paper towel until cold.
  4. Heat up the fryer until 180 degrees Celcius.
  5. Fry the cold fries until golden brown.
  6. Add the fries back to the bowl with a piece of paper towel and sprinkle generously with salt.
