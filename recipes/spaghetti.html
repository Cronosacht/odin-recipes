Vlaamse spaghetti bolognese

Description

Spaghetti bolognese is a traditional Italian tomato and meat based pasta dish. Every household has its own version of this dish. This is my version.

Ingredients

For 4 people:

  • Spaghetti 500 gr
  • Tomato puree 280 gr
  • Ground pork-veal 1 kg
  • Large onions 2
  • Red bell pepper 2
  • Yellow bell pepper 2
  • Ducros spaghettikruiden mix 2 tbsp
  • Ducros spaghettikruiden hot 1 tbsp
  • Dried bay leaf 4
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Dice the onions and cut the bell peppers in long strips.
  2. Heat up a lump of butter in a large pot on medium-high heat and add the bell peppers. Let them cook for about 8 minutes and stir once in a while.
  3. Add the diced onions and let cook for another 5 minutes. When done, empty the pot into a bowl.
  4. Heat up some more butter in the same pot and add the ground meat. Squash the meat into tiny pieces.
  5. Add the onions and bell peppers back to the pot. Also add the tomato puree and herbs. Stir well and let it gently simmer for about 45 minutes.
  6. Cook the spaghetti according to the instruction on the package. Serve in a bowl with a generous amount of sauce and some grated cheese to your liking.
