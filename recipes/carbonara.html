Spaghetti alla carbonara

Description

Spaghetti alla carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with a fatty cured pork, hard cheese, eggs, salt and black pepper.

The dish is often wrongly made with cream, but this recipe will guide you through the authentic way of making it.

Ingredients

For 4 people

  • Spaghetti 500 gr
  • Guanchiale (Cured pork) 250 gr
  • Eggs 5
  • Pecorino Romano (cheese) 200 gr
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Put on a large cooking pot with water on the stove and bring to the boil. Add a big snuff of salt to the water.
  2. Put a frying pan on the the stove and heat a dash of olive oil in it.
  3. Cut the guanchiale in cubes and fry in the hot pan. Fry until crispy, but stir regularly.
  4. Grate the Pecorino Romana. Take a bowl and break the eggs in it. Whisk the eggs and add the grated cheese while whisking.
  5. Add the spaghetti to the pot as soon as the water is boiling. Cook the pasta al dente
  6. Drain the pasta and add it to a large bowl.
  7. Add the mixture of eggs and cheese to the bowl of pasta and stir continuously.
  8. Add the crunchy pieces of guanciale and season well with black pepper.
  9. Stir the bowl a final time and serve the pasta in bowls with some more grated cheese on top.
